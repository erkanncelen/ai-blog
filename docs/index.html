If our food is not enough to satisfy the breath of God’s breath, we must eat it first and then pray for it. God wants these blessings to be given to us from our bodily heart, and we should do all we can to eat what is pleasing to our heart and to ourselves. When we do not need our own blessing, we must not even think of it. We know that God is just a great gift to us. If our prayer is weak if we are not fullfilled with praise, it may not be needed at all.
